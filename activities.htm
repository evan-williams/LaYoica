Activities

There are a number of activities to enjoy, both in the farm and the surrounding area. Lago Puelo and the whole of the valley of the 42nd Parallel, as it is also known, has an amazing array of natural beauty to explore. There is something to enjoy in all the seasons, from the warm summer days, to the unbelievable colours of Autumn, the new blossoms and new life of spring to the snow capped mountains of winter.

Wonder leisurely around the farm, the pond, sleep under the fruit trees, listen to the quiet of nature, the birds. The little ones can play in the treehouse, also fully equipped with all kitchen utensils, as well as explore their surroundings.

We have developed for school groups, a very large number of activities for children of all ages to learn to appreciate nature, and if your young ones are keen, we can suggest some games and activities for you to do together as a family.

For a slightly more energetic stroll, and for panoramic views of the valley in all directions - north to the Rio Azul Valley, and to the south, Lago Puelo and the Tres Picos Mountains - walk up the mountain to explore the nature trails we have created.

They have been thoughtfully planned to get the most out of all the views and to showcase all the different autochthonous plant species and natural wonders in this Nature Reserve.

You will take our information leaflet to guide you, which point out things of interest along the way. Take a picnic and relax in one of the two “Refugios”, built to frame the panoramic views.

Our dogs will accompany you and show you the way if you want, but we can also take you if you want company.

Longer walks outside the farm perimeter will take you to the Rio Quemquemtreu and onto the Azul, where you can enjoy a spot of fishing or swim in the waterholes.

We can offer suggestions and guidance on many of the varied activities this area has to offer, such as: Cabalgatas Lago Puelo.

There are longer walks to some unforgettable places, including the refuges at different points in the high mountains such as the Cajon De Azul.
