About Us

We arrived here in 1976, with a newly born baby and dreams of bringing up our family here. Eduardo had just graduated as a Forest Engineer in Buenos Aires, and Jean had left her native Scotland behind to build a new life together in El Bolson.La Yoica has been our home for over 40 years,we brought up our 4 girls here, and it has come a very long way from the wild thorn covered land we initially started with.

We have always maintained the focus of protecting the environment which we feel extremely lucky to live in, both for our family and to offer our visitors a truly unique and unforgettable experience. All the trees we have planted, the native woods we have protected, turning the farm into a Nature Reserve, and every single thing we do comes from a true love for nature and it's magic. Let us share it with you.
